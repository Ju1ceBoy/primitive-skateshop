Our Team & Our Trophies

Our team consists of world-class professional athletes. Each of them managed to prove himself and annually take away dozens of awards and medals.


The main team of the world league includes such famous riders as Shane O'Neil, Nyjah Huston, Chris Coll, Tiago Lemos, Carlos Ribeiro, Giovanni Vianna, Paul Rodriguez, Wade Desarmo, Miles Silvas.


In addition to world legends and high-class skaters, every year we open a recruitment for our young team. The basis of which you can get. Every young skater who wants to prove himself has repeatedly been to such big competitions as: SLS, Tampa, X-Games, etc.


Every year we do everything to improve our results and for this we provide each of our athletes with constant sports equipment, such as: accessories for skateboards, trips and accommodation at the expense of the organization, constant meetings with skateboarding legends, free, and most importantly, the safest skateparks, etc.
